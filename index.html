Nearby Adventures

5 MIN DRIVE

Port Franks Beach

Access our private community beach. Famous for its quiet sandy shores and incredible Lake Huron sunsets.

5 MIN DRIVE

Sand Hills Golf Course

A beautiful and unique 18-hole course carved into the natural dunes and Carolinian forest.

15 MIN DRIVE

Grand Bend Strip

The famous main street with eclectic shops, nightlife, restaurants, and the iconic Grand Bend public beach.

10 MIN DRIVE

Pinery Provincial Park

A nature lover's paradise. Enjoy miles of hiking, biking trails, and canoeing on the Old Ausable Channel.

WINERIES

Sip & Sample

Visit the nearby Shale Ridge Estate Winery or Dark Horse Estate Winery for tastings and locally sourced food.

LOCAL EATS

Grog’s Pub & Grill

A legendary local spot. Famous for their pizzas, burgers, and the must-try "Zaney Bread".
