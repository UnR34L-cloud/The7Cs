Nearby Adventures

5 MIN DRIVE

Port Franks Private Beach

Exclusive access to one of Lake Huron's quietest sandy beaches. Perfect for sunset walks and peaceful swimming.

5 MIN DRIVE

Sand Hills Golf Course

Test your skills on this beautiful 18-hole course. Known for its challenging elevations and scenery.

15 MIN DRIVE

The Grand Bend Strip

The heart of summer fun. Explore dozens of boutiques, bars, restaurants, and the iconic main beach.

10 MIN DRIVE

Pinery Provincial Park

A provincial treasure featuring rare oak savanna ecosystems, endless hiking trails, and canoeing.

WINERY TOURS

Shale Ridge & Dark Horse

Enjoy local cider and wine tastings. Many guests recommend taking the short scenic drive to these awards-winning estates.

LOCAL FAVORITE

Grog’s Pub & Grill

A Port Franks staple for over 30 years. You cannot leave without trying their famous Zaney Bread and signature pizzas.
